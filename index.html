About Us

DSB Documentation Services is a premier documentation and legal processing company based in Naga City. We specialize in land documentation, legal processing, taxation services, and government transactions — making complex bureaucratic processes simple, fast, and stress-free for our clients.

Government Agencies

Offices We Work With

We process your documents across all major government agencies, ensuring compliance and accuracy at every step.

ROD

Register of Deeds

Title transfers, annotations, cancellations, and all certificate of title transactions.

LRA

Land Registration Authority

Original registration, reconstitution of lost titles, and issuance of owner's duplicate.

DAR

Dept. of Agrarian Reform

Land conversion clearances, CLOA processing, and agrarian reform compliance documents.

DENR

Dept. of Environment & Natural Resources

Lot plans, survey returns, environmental clearances, and public land applications.

AO

Assessor's Office

Tax declarations, real property unit assessments, and property classification processing.

TO

Treasury Office

Real property tax clearances, tax payment processing, and certification requests.
